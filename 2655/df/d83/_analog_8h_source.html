28 /* Analog class is dedicated to perform fair emulation of analogue circuitry of hardware units that is responsible
29  * for processing output signal after the DAC. It appears that the analogue circuit labeled "LPF" on the schematic
30  * also applies audible changes to the signal spectra. There is a significant boost of higher frequencies observed
31  * aside from quite poor attenuation of the mirror spectra above 16 kHz which is due to a relatively low filter order.
32  *
33  * As the final mixing of multiplexed output signal is performed after the DAC, this function is migrated here from Synth.
34  * Saying precisely, mixing is performed within the LPF as the entrance resistors are actually components of a LPF
35  * designed using the multiple feedback topology. Nevertheless, the schematic separates them.
36  */
